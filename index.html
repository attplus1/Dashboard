Overview

Round-trip trades are reconstructed from your broker history: entries (Buy/Sell Trade) are matched to exits (Close Trade / Stop Loss) by order reference, with stop-loss exits matched FIFO per ticker. Realised P&L is read from the statement and is shown gross of commission and overnight funding — those are tracked separately and combined into the Net P&L figures. Use the date range to scope every metric and chart below.

Metric Definitions

Momentum Screener

Ranks the ASX universe using Jegadeesh & Titman (1993) cross-sectional momentum: the formation return over the prior 6 months, skipping the most recent month (a "6–1" lookback) to avoid short-term reversal. The strongest names form the suggested buy list for the coming period. Each card shows a daily candlestick chart with 50- and 200-day moving averages.
